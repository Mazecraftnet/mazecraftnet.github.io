Our Gamemodes

Discover unique worlds and adventures across our diverse game modes.

Wanderlight SMP

Embark on an epic adventure through a world where ancient secrets, mythical creatures, and untamed landscapes come to life. Wanderlight Realms blends immersive exploration, challenging battles, and vibrant biomes to create a captivating fantasy experience.

Unlimited Weaponworks

Command, create, and conquer with Create Weaponworks! This Create-powered modpack invites you to unleash your engineering prowess in a world where warfare meets intricate contraptions. Build tanks, deploy powerful cannons, craft advanced technologies, and lead your forces to victory in Minecraft’s most innovative war simulation.

Industrial Horizons

Unleash your creativity and dominate automation with Industrial Horizons! This Create-powered modpack lets you dive into a world where engineering meets imagination. From automating everything to mining everything, and even embarking on an interplanetary journey, the possibilities are endless.
